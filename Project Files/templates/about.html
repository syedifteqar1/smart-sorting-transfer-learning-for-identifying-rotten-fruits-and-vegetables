About NutriGaze

NutriGaze is a pioneering organization dedicated to enhancing the quality and safety of your fruits and vegetables using smart technology.

Our mission is to assist farmers, vendors, and consumers in identifying spoilage and ripeness in produce using deep learning and image classification.

  • Classifies fruits and vegetables as healthy or rotten.
  • Helps reduce food waste by early detection of spoilage.
  • Ensures quality and freshness from farm to plate.
